## New Testament

- neither circumcision availeth any thing, Gal. 5:6.
- fervent prayer of a righteous man availeth much, James 5:16.

## Book of Mormon

- law of Moses availeth nothing except, Mosiah 3:15.
- unto such baptism availeth nothing, Moro. 8:22.
